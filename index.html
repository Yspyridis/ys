YANNIS SPYRIDIS


Postdoctoral Researcher - Software Engineer

About

Yannis Spyridis is a dedicated researcher with a Ph.D from the University of Sheffield, specialising in autonomous drone control. He has actively been involved in multiple European research projects, supported by the Horizon programme. His primary research focuses on AI applications and multi-agent optimisation. Currently, he works as a postdoctoral researcher in Kingston University London.

In addition to his academic pursuits, he has extensive experience as a software engineer, specialising in both backend and frontend development, REST API design, and data analysis and visualisation. He has a strong track record of successfully applying Python to develop user-friendly web applications and build AI solutions, making valuable contributions to data-driven projects.
